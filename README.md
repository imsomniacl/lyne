# Premium Bilingual Law Firm Website

This repository contains the source code, CMS configuration, documentation, and implementation plan for a premium bilingual law firm website focused on Chile, especially the corridor from Concepción to Puerto Montt.

The site is designed to:
- generate qualified consultations and inbound leads
- support a WhatsApp-first contact flow
- publish legal updates, guides, and articles for SEO and AEO
- remain easy to update through a visual CMS
- grow organically without exposing unfinished content

## Project goals

The website should position the firm as:
- corporate and premium first
- close and human second
- commercially intelligent
- practical and strategic

Primary legal focus:
- Civil Law / Business Law
- Labor Law
- Tax And Tributes

Secondary legal areas may exist in the CMS but remain hidden from the public until ready:
- Penal Law
- Administrative Law
- Family Law

## Primary audience

Priority order:
1. established companies
2. foreign investors
3. startups
4. HR teams

Secondary audience:
- high income individuals in strategically relevant legal matters

## Geographic focus

Primary regional focus:
- Concepción
- Los Ángeles
- Temuco
- Valdivia
- Osorno
- Puerto Varas
- Puerto Montt

Wider relevance:
- Biobío
- La Araucanía
- Los Ríos
- Los Lagos

International support:
- English pages for clients from Canada, the United States, and Europe

## Core product rules

- Spanish is the primary language
- English is secondary
- WhatsApp general chat is the primary CTA
- Calendar booking is the secondary CTA
- Hidden or incomplete content must not be visible publicly
- The site must support staging preview before production release
- The public site must look complete and intentional at all times

## Recommended stack

- Next.js App Router
- TypeScript
- Sanity CMS
- Vercel
- Google Analytics
- Google Search Console

Optional later:
- Cloudflare
- CRM integration
- advanced reporting tools
